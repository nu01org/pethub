# PetHub

PetHub is a modern pet care platform that connects pet owners, veterinarians, shelters, and caregivers in one place. From health tracking and smart monitoring to adoption management, PetHub helps ensure pets live healthier, safer, and happier lives.

# Features

* 🐾 Pet Profiles — Manage detailed pet information, including breed, age, medical history, and identification records.
* ❤️ Health Management — Track vaccinations, medications, vet visits, allergies, and wellness milestones.
* 📷 Camera Monitoring — Monitor pet presence, activity, and behavior through connected cameras and smart devices.
* 🏠 Adoption Management — Streamline adoption applications, approvals, pet matching, and post-adoption follow-up.
* 🔔 Reminders & Alerts — Receive notifications for medications, vaccinations, appointments, and daily care routines.
* 👨‍⚕️ Veterinary Integration — Maintain and share health records with veterinary professionals.
* 📍 Location & Safety — Keep pets safe with location tracking and lost-pet support features.
* 📊 Activity Insights — Monitor exercise, sleep, feeding habits, and behavioral trends.
* 👥 Shared Care — Collaborate with family members, pet sitters, shelters, and veterinarians.
